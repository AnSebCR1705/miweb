## 🌱 Origin & Purpose

This project has its origins in the Data Mining course made by the Dr. Vlada Pleshcheva and taught at the Faculty of Sciences and Engineering of th Pontifical University Catholic of Peru during the 2025-2 term. The original content of the course was taught in the statistical R language and is the property of both the university and the professor who was responsible of the course; therefore, the shared teaching material in this repository provides the datasets used during the academic cycle, as well as the reimplmentation of the methodology used in the course, adapted to the Python programming language.

> 🎯 The purpose of this repository is to be viewed by students interesed in learning about data science and so that they can transfer their knowledge of data analytics and machine learning techniques applied in R to Python, and to allow them to find the insights related to the differences between both languages.
